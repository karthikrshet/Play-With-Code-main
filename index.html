About

Play with Code is an exciting coding event designed to inspire and challenge participants of all skill levels to explore the world of programming. Through a series of fun, hands-on challenges and problem-solving activities, participants will engage in creative coding tasks, sharpen their technical skills, and collaborate with like-minded individuals.Play with Code offers a unique opportunity to learn, innovate, and have fun while exploring the limitless possibilities of code!
